# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name
YouTube Safety Inspector

## Short Description (132 char max)
Detects dangerous DIY, medical & cooking advice in YouTube videos. Flags AI-generated content. Suggests safe alternatives.

## Detailed Description

YouTube Safety Inspector analyzes videos for potentially dangerous content and AI-generated misinformation.

**What it detects:**

- Dangerous DIY advice (galvanized metal for cooking, PVC for compressed air)
- Unsafe fitness instructions (locked knees, extreme back arching)
- Hazardous cooking practices (water in hot oil, undercooked poultry)
- Dangerous medical claims (bleach cures, essential oil treatments)
- Chemical mixing dangers (bleach + ammonia, water in acid)
- Unsafe childcare advice (crib bedding, infant honey)
- AI-generated or deepfake video content

**Features:**

- Safety score from 0-100 for each video
- Detailed warnings with severity levels
- Safe alternative suggestions from trusted sources
- AI content detection with confidence indicators
- Trusted channel allowlist
- Configurable sensitivity levels
- Keyboard shortcuts (Esc, I, Shift+A)
- Works on regular videos and Shorts

**Privacy:**

- No personal data collected
- Analysis performed locally + optional API
- No third-party tracking
- Settings stored locally in your browser

**Open Source:**
This extension is open source under the MIT license.
